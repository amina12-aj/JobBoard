About Us

Tenece Professional Services Limited (Tenece) is a multi-competency enterprise transformation and technology-consulting firm that combines expertise and capabilities across many industries and business functions to deliver superior value to its clients.Tenece collaborates with its clients, both in the private and public sectors, to build high performance enterprises.Tenece principals and directors have a combined industry experience of over 40 years in various sectors of the economy: with competence in business transformation, financial advisory, software engineering, communication technology, e-Business development, government transformations (e-Government), systems integration, IT consulting, and process reengineering. We are team of closely knitted people who believe in high performance as well as fun. Working in Tenece is working for your personal and career development.

Our Vision: To be the foremost enterprise technology consulting services firm in Nigeria and Africa by 2030.
Our Mission: To continually collaborate with our esteemed stakeholders to meet desired business objectives by providing first-class, unique, premium, cost-effective solutions.

Our Core Values

High Performance: We are proactive and take the initiative in meeting and surpassing our goals which bring incremental value to our people and business.
