About Us

Tenece Professional Services Limited (Tenece) is a multi-competency enterprise transformation and technology-consulting firm that combines expertise and capabilities across many industries and business functions to deliver superior value to its clients.Tenece collaborates with its clients, both in the private and public sectors, to build high performance enterprises. We are team of closely knitted people who believe in high performance as well as fun. Working in Tenece is working for your personal and career development.
Our Vision:
To be the foremost enterprise technology consulting services firm in Nigeria and Africa by 2030.
Our Mission:
To continually collaborate with our esteemed stakeholders to meet desired business objectives by providing first-class, unique, premium, cost-effective solutions.
